About Me

Thank you for visiting my portfolio! I am a recent graduate of Graphic and Interactive Design from the Harrisburg Area Community College in Harrisburg Pennsylvania, and have a background in research biology and education. I have a side business, Claire Lorts Designs, designing and crafting sterling silver and wooden jewelry in addition to lapidary work.

I am thrilled to start a new chapter in graphic design and have interests in packaging design and problem solving through visual communication, and maintain a flexible mindset regarding my future direction in the field.

I live in Pennsylvania with my husband and cat, and enjoy visiting family in Michigan and Nevada! Please do not hesitate to contact me with any questions!
